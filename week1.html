Introduction and Setting up Week

Indroduction

Pre Fab Academy Programme, Kerala Startup Mission in association with Fab foundation is conducted a one month training programme exclusively for the makers in Kerala. The workshop held from 19th Oct to 13th Nov at Fablab Cochin.The programme includes hands on training on all the machines in fab lab as well as introduction to version control,Documentaion, electronics, programming, molding, casting, design softwares and Conferencing setup and etiquette.
